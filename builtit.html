Build It

Team Formation

  • Each team can have 2–4 participants.

Objective

  • Teams must construct a model structure (Bridge / Beam / Tower) using the provided materials.

Materials Provided

  • Ice cream sticks and basic joining materials (as specified) will be provided by organizers.
  • No external materials are allowed.

Time Limit

  • Construction must be completed within the given time (e.g., 45–60 minutes).

Design Flexibility

  • Participants are free to choose any design or type (Bridge, Beam, or Tower).
  • Creativity and innovation are encouraged.

Construction Constraints

  • The structure must be built within the specified dimensions/area.
  • Pre-made parts are not allowed.

Testing Phase

  • After construction, the structure will undergo load testing.
  • For bridges/beams → load will be applied.
  • For towers → height and stability will be tested.

Evaluation Criteria

  • Strength and load-bearing capacity.
  • Stability and durability.
  • Design efficiency.
  • Creativity and neatness.

Winning Criteria

  • The team whose structure performs best under testing (maximum load/stability/height) will be declared the winner.

Disqualification

  • Use of unauthorized materials or violation of rules will lead to disqualification.

Judgement

  • Judges’ decision will be final and binding.
