🏗 BUILD IT

Engineer the Structure. Prove the Strength.

👥 Team Size

2 – 4 Members

⏱ Duration

45 – 60 Minutes

🧰 Materials

Ice Cream Sticks & Glue

🏗 Structure

Beam / Bridge / Building

Event Description

Build It is a hands-on structural design competition that challenges participants to apply engineering fundamentals, creativity, and teamwork.

Teams will construct a model structure using limited materials within a fixed time frame. The focus is on strength, stability, efficiency, and smart structural planning.

Participants must enter as a team and construct a Beam, Bridge, or Building model that will later be tested for load-bearing capacity and stability.

Round Details

Phase 1 – Technical Quiz

All registered teams will participate in a technical quiz to test their core engineering knowledge. Top-performing teams will qualify for the construction round.

Phase 2 – Construction Challenge

Qualified teams must design and build a Beam / Bridge / Building model using the provided materials within 45–60 minutes.

After construction, structures will undergo load and stability testing to evaluate their performance.

Guidelines

  • No external materials allowed.
  • Structures must be built within specified dimensions.
  • Pre-made or pre-assembled components are strictly prohibited.
  • Teams may build Beam, Bridge, or Building structures.
  • Creativity and innovation are encouraged.

Evaluation Parameters

  • Load-bearing capacity
  • Stability and durability
  • Design efficiency
  • Creativity and neatness

⚠ Violation of rules will lead to disqualification.

Judges’ decision will be final and binding.
