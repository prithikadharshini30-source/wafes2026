Rigid Retainer

Team Formation

  • Each team can have 2–4 participants.

Objective

  • Participants must construct a retaining wall model using the materials provided.

Materials

  • Required materials (bricks/blocks, base, etc.) will be provided.

Time Limit

  • Teams must complete construction within the specified time (e.g., 45–60 minutes).

Design Freedom

  • Teams can choose any suitable retaining wall design within the given materials.

Construction Rules

  • The wall must be built within the allocated area/dimensions.
  • Use of external support or fixing methods (unless provided) is not allowed.

Testing Phase

  • After construction, a load/stability test will be conducted.
  • The wall will be subjected to lateral load/pressure simulation.

Evaluation Criteria

  • Stability and strength of the wall.
  • Ability to retain against applied load.
  • Structural efficiency and neatness.

Winning Criteria

  • The team whose wall withstands the maximum load or remains stable without failure will be declared the winner.

Disqualification

  • Use of unauthorized materials or unfair practices will lead to disqualification.

Judgement

  • Judges’ decision will be final and binding.
