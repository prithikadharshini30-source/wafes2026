🧱 RIGID RETAINER

Design the Wall. Resist the Load.

👥 Team Size

2 – 4 Members

⏱ Duration

45 – 60 Minutes

🏗 Event Type

Structural Challenge

🧱 Structure

Retaining Wall Model

Event Description

Rigid Retainer is a structural challenge where participants apply engineering principles to design and construct a retaining wall model.

Teams must build a stable structure using the provided materials and demonstrate its ability to resist lateral pressure during testing.

Round Details

Phase 1 – Technical Quiz

All registered teams will participate in a technical quiz to test their fundamental engineering knowledge. Top-performing teams will qualify for the construction round.

Phase 2 – Retaining Wall Construction

Qualified teams must construct a retaining wall model using the provided materials within 45–60 minutes.

After construction, the wall will undergo a load and stability test where lateral pressure will be applied to evaluate performance.

Guidelines

  • Team Size: 2–4 members
  • Required materials such as bricks/blocks, base, and specified items will be provided.
  • No external materials are allowed.
  • The wall must be built within the allocated area or specified dimensions.
  • External supports or fixing methods (unless provided) are not permitted.
  • Teams may choose any suitable retaining wall design using the given materials.

Evaluation Parameters

  • Stability and strength of the wall
  • Ability to retain against applied load
  • Structural efficiency and neatness

The team whose wall withstands the maximum load or remains stable without failure will be declared the winner.

⚠ Violation of rules or use of unauthorized materials will lead to disqualification.

Judges’ decision will be final and binding.
