Event Description

Clue Chase is an exciting problem-solving event where teams follow a trail of hidden clues to reach the final destination.

The challenge tests participants’ logical thinking, observation skills, and teamwork.

Round Details

Phase 1 – Puzzle Quiz

Teams will solve a set of puzzles to qualify for the next round.

Phase 2 – Clue Hunt

Teams will receive the first clue and must solve a sequence of clues hidden within the designated area. Each clue will guide them to the next location until the final clue is reached.

Guidelines

  • Team Size: 2–4 members
  • Clues will be hidden only within the assigned area.
  • Searching outside the permitted zone is not allowed.
  • Clues must be left in their original place after reading.
  • Clues must not be damaged or removed.
  • Teams must maintain fair play and should not disturb or follow other teams.
  • Limited hints may be provided with a penalty.
  • The team that completes the clue sequence in the shortest time will be declared the winner.

Judges’ decision will be final and binding.
