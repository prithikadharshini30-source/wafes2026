Clue Chase

Team Formation

  • Each team can have 2–4 participants.

Objective

  • Teams must find and solve a series of clues hidden within the designated area to reach the final clue.

Game Format

  • The first clue will be provided at the start.
  • Each clue will lead to the next location.
  • Teams must follow the sequence correctly.

Search Area

  • Clues will be hidden only within the assigned room/area.
  • Participants must not search outside the permitted zone.

Time Limit

  • The event will have a fixed time limit.

Clue Handling

  • Clues must be left in their original place after reading.
  • Do not damage or remove any clue.

Fair Play

  • Teams must not disturb, mislead, or follow other teams.
  • Any form of unfair practice leads to disqualification.

Hints

  • Limited hints may be provided with a penalty (optional).

Winning Criteria

  • The team that finds all clues and reaches the final point in the shortest time will be declared the winner.

Tie-Breaker

  • In case of a tie, the team with fewer hints used or faster last clue completion wins.
