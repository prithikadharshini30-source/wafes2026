Poster & Reel Showcase

Team Formation

  • Each team can have 1–4 participants.

Objective

  • Participants must present a digital poster or short reel based on the given theme.

Submission & Presentation

  • Entries must be created digitally.
  • Participants must showcase their work using laptops or mobile phones.
  • No physical charts or printed materials are required.

Poster Category

Format

  • Digital poster (JPEG/PNG/PDF format).
  • Clear visuals and readable text.

Rules

  • Poster must clearly reflect the theme.
  • Content should be original.
  • Plagiarism or copied designs will lead to disqualification.

Reel Category

Duration

  • Reel must be within 30–60 seconds.

Format

  • Vertical format (9:16 preferred).
  • Clear audio and visuals required.

Rules

  • Content must strictly follow the theme.
  • Use of inappropriate or copyrighted content is not allowed.
  • Reel must be original and created by the team.

Evaluation Criteria

For Poster:

  • Creativity and originality
  • Relevance to theme
  • Visual impact and clarity
  • Effectiveness of message

For Reel:

  • Creativity and storytelling
  • Technical quality
  • Engagement and impact
  • Relevance to theme

Winning Criteria

  • The team with the highest total score in their respective category will be declared the winner.

Disqualification

  • Use of unfair practices or plagiarism will lead to disqualification.

Judgement

  • Judges’ decision will be final and binding.
