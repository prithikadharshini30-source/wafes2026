Image IQ

Team Formation

  • Each team can have 2–4 participants.

Objective

  • Participants must identify the common connection or concept linking a set of given images.

Materials

  • Images will be displayed on screen.
  • Answer sheets will be provided.
  • No additional materials or electronic devices are allowed.

Time Limit

  • Each question must be answered within the specified time (e.g., 60–90 seconds).

Event Format

  • Each round will contain 3–5 related images.
  • Teams must identify the correct connection between them.

Rules

  • Answers must be clear and specific.
  • Discussion is allowed only within the team.
  • No external help is permitted.

Evaluation Criteria

  • Accuracy of the answer
  • Speed of response
  • Logical correctness

Winning Criteria

  • The team with the highest total score at the end of all rounds will be declared the winner.

Disqualification

  • Use of unauthorized materials or unfair practices will lead to disqualification.

Judgement

  • Judges’ decision will be final and binding.
