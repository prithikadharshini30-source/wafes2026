🏆 Goal

Event Description

Image IQ is a visual reasoning challenge where teams analyze a set of images and identify the common concept or connection linking them.

The event tests observation skills, logical thinking, and quick decision-making. Participants must carefully study visual clues and determine the correct link between images.

Round Details

Phase 1 – Memory Round

Teams will be shown a set of images for a limited time. Participants must observe and remember them carefully to answer related questions.

Phase 2 – Image Connection Round

Teams will be given a group of images and must identify the common concept or connection linking them.

Guidelines

  • Team Size: 2–4 members
  • Images will be displayed on the screen and answer sheets will be provided.
  • Electronic devices or external help are not permitted.
  • Discussion is allowed only within the team.
  • The team with the highest total score will be declared the winner.

Judges’ decision will be final and binding.
