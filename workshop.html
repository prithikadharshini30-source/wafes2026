🏗 Topic

Post-Tension Systems

Event Description

This workshop focuses on providing an understanding of the Post-Tensioning System, an advanced prestressing technique widely used in modern structural engineering.

Participants will gain insight into the principles, components, and structural advantages of post-tensioned systems and their applications in contemporary construction.

Workshop Session

The session will cover key concepts including:

  • Fundamentals of prestressed concrete
  • Principle of post-tensioning and induced compressive stresses
  • Stress distribution and load transfer mechanism
  • Components such as high-tensile steel tendons, anchor heads, wedges, and ducts
  • Flexural behavior and deflection control
  • Crack mitigation and serviceability performance
  • Comparison between conventional RCC and post-tensioned structures
  • Applications in slabs, beams, bridge decks, and high-rise buildings

Duration: 45 minutes to 1 hour.
