Event Description

Relay of Task is a fast-paced team challenge where participants complete a series of tasks in a relay format.

Each team member performs a task sequentially, testing the team's coordination, speed, and problem-solving ability.

Round Details

Phase 1 – Reverse Quiz

Teams will participate in a reverse quiz where answers are provided and participants must identify the correct question or concept.

Phase 2 – Relay Challenge

Teams must complete a sequence of tasks in relay order, where each member performs one task at a time within the given time limit.

Guidelines

  • Team Size: 2–4 members. No substitution once the event begins.
  • Tasks must be completed strictly in relay order.
  • Only one participant is allowed in the task zone at a time.
  • The next member can begin only after the previous member finishes their task.
  • Mobile phones, smart devices, or external help are not permitted.
  • Teams must follow the instructions of coordinators and judges.
  • Any malpractice or rule violation will lead to disqualification.

Judges’ decision will be final and binding.
