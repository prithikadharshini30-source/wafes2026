Relay of Task

1. Team Structure

  • Each team must consist of 2-4 members.
  • No substitution of members once the event begins.
  • All members must be present at the reporting time.

2. Relay Format

  • Tasks must be completed strictly in relay order.
  • Only one participant is allowed in the task zone at a time.
  • The next member can begin only after the previous member fully completes their task.
  • Skipping tasks is not allowed.

3. Time Regulations

  • Total event time: 15 minutes.
  • Exceeding the time limit results in penalty time addition.
  • If the team fails to complete all tasks within the total time, they will be disqualified.

4. Discipline & Fair Play

  • No use of mobile phones, smart devices, or external help.
  • No communication with audience or other teams during the performance.
  • Any form of malpractice leads to immediate disqualification.
  • Teams must follow the instructions of coordinators and judges without argument.

5. Safety & Conduct

  • Any damage to materials or misbehavior will result in elimination.
  • The decision of the judges is final and binding.
  • In case of disputes, the organizing committee holds authority.
