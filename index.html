About WAFES 2026

WAFES 2026 is the premier Civil Technical Symposium hosted by the Department of Civil Engineering, Meenakshi Sundararajan Engineering College. Named after the five fundamental elements – Water, Air, Fire, Earth, and Space – WAFES represents the essence of civil engineering and its role in shaping the built environment.


This symposium serves as a dynamic platform where aspiring engineers, researchers, and industry professionals converge to exchange knowledge, showcase innovations, and explore sustainable solutions for the future of construction and infrastructure.
