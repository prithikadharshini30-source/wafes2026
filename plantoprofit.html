Plan to Profit

Design. Strategize. Monetize.

Plan to Profit is a design and strategy competition where teams are given a specific plot area and must conceptualize a profit-generating building within a limited time.

Participants are expected to create a feasible design, plan space utilization, and develop a basic revenue model to justify profitability.

The focus is on smart planning, practicality, and business thinking.

Round Details

Phase 1: Design & Business Planning (60 Minutes)

  • Design a suitable structure for the given plot.
  • Prepare a simple cost and revenue model showing expected profit.

Phase 2: Presentation

  • Present your concept, planning approach, and profit strategy before the judges.

Guidelines

  • Team size: 1–4 members.
  • Any design software (AutoCAD, SketchUp, Revit, etc.), productivity software (Excel, PowerPoint, etc.), AI tools, internet resources, and hand-drawn sketches are allowed.
  • Presentation can be in any format including digital models, slides, drawings, charts, or sketches.
  • All required materials and equipment must be brought by the team.
  • Only limited power supply will be provided; teams must come prepared accordingly.
  • Judges’ decision will be final.
